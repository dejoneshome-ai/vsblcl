Quick questions, answered.

Is this a subscription or a contract?
No. The snapshot is free, the Visibility Package is £75 once, and the website is £395 once. No subscriptions, no contracts, no strings.
What if I don't like the Visibility Package?
You don't pay. You see the full thing first — there's no awkward conversation, no chasing, no obligation either way.
Do I need to do anything technical?
No. You send your business name and town by email or phone, I do the work, you get the result. For a website, I handle everything from the first draft to going live.
How long does it actually take?
Your Visibility Package arrives within 48 hours of sending your details. A full website is live in about two weeks. I take on a handful of businesses at a time so each one gets proper attention rather than being rushed.
How do I pay?
For the £75 package: only after you've seen it and you're happy, through a secure PayPal link (you don't need a PayPal account). For a website, we'll agree the details when we talk.
How is this different to an agency?
No retainer, no minimum term, no account manager — just one person doing the work properly for a fair, one-off price. If what you need is genuinely agency-sized, I'll say so.
