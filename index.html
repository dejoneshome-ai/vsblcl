Quick questions, answered.

Is this a subscription or a contract?
Your website is a one-off — £350 to £1,100 — and it's yours to keep, no contract. The snapshot is free. The only recurring thing is the optional care plan (£15–£75/month), which you choose to add, can cancel any time, and never have to take at all.
How does the Direct Booking System avoid commission?
The booking happens on your own website, not on a platform. Guests pick dates from your live calendar and pay by card straight to you. There's no OTA taking a 15% cut — you only pay the standard card processing fee, the same as any shop.
Do I have to leave Booking.com or Airbnb?
Not at all. Plenty of hosts keep the OTAs for reach and use direct booking for repeat guests and anyone who finds them on Google — where there's no commission. Every booking you move to direct is money back in your pocket.
Do I need to do anything technical?
No. You send your business name and town, I do the work, you get the result. For a website and booking system I handle everything from first draft to going live — you just manage your calendar with a couple of taps.
How long does it actually take?
A paid audit arrives within 48 hours. A full website, with direct bookings if you want them, is live in about two weeks. I take on a handful of businesses at a time so each gets proper attention.
How do I pay?
A paid audit is a secure PayPal link once it's done (no PayPal account needed). For a website and booking system, we'll agree the details when we talk. A care plan, if you choose one, is a simple monthly payment you can cancel any time.
