A VisibleCymru product · Direct Booking System

Take the booking yourself. Keep the whole night.

The Direct Booking System is your own availability calendar and secure checkout, built into your website — so guests book and pay you directly, and the commission stays yours.

  • Real-time availability calendar

    Guests see what's actually free and book it on the spot. You block dates in a couple of taps — no double-bookings.

  • Secure card payments

    Deposits or full payment taken safely at the point of booking, straight into your account. Guest details captured cleanly.

  • Mobile-first guest flow

    Most guests book from a phone on the sofa. The whole flow — dates, details, pay — is fast and effortless on a small screen.

  • Zero OTA commission

    No 15% skim, no per-booking fee to a platform. What the guest pays is what you keep, minus only the card processing.

Quick questions, answered.

Is this a subscription or a contract?
No. The snapshot is free, the Visibility Package is £75 once, and the website is £395 once. The Direct Booking System is a one-off add-on to your build. No subscriptions, no contracts.
How does the Direct Booking System avoid commission?
Because the booking happens on your own website, not on a platform. Guests pick their dates from your live calendar and pay by card straight to you. There's no OTA taking a 15% cut — you only pay the standard card processing fee, the same as any shop.
Do I have to leave Booking.com or Airbnb?
Not at all. Plenty of hosts keep the OTAs for reach and use direct booking for repeat guests and anyone who finds them on Google — where there's no commission to pay. Every booking you move to direct is money back in your pocket.
Do I need to do anything technical?
No. You send your business name and town, I do the work, you get the result. For a website and booking system I handle everything from the first draft to going live — you just manage your calendar with a couple of taps.
How long does it actually take?
Your Visibility Package arrives within 48 hours. A full website, with direct bookings if you want them, is live in about two weeks. I take on a handful of businesses at a time so each gets proper attention.
How do I pay?
For the £75 package: only after you've seen it and you're happy, through a secure PayPal link (no PayPal account needed). For a website and booking system, we'll agree the details when we talk.
